** Microsoft Software Caused Data Breach **

What: 
    -- Chinese hacking group attacked Microsoft and affected over 30,000 orgs (including govs and gov agencies).
    -- Targeting "entities in the US for the purpose of exfiltrating info from a number of industry sectors"

Flaws: 
    -- stolen passwords
    -- previously undetected vulnerabilities on servers running MS Exchange software.
        -- This alloed any user who had physical or virtual access at time of login to gain full administrative rights. 

Resolution:
    -- MS sent communication to customers to immediately install all software patches for their systems.
